360 view

An AI-powered 360-degree panoramic view will generate a virtual tour of the renovated apartment. Potential buyers can thoroughly explore the living spaces before finalizing the project with the architect.

Multiple architecturals plans

Correlating multiple architectural plans reduces supply chain costs. By integrating various designs, inefficiencies are identified and streamlined, optimizing material sourcing, transportation, and logistics, leading to cost savings
